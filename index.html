Who We Are

At Smile Heart Ghana, we are a dedicated team of young individuals united by a shared vision: to create a better Ghana for orphans and those in need. Our organization is registered under the Companies Act, 2019 (Act 992) with a Certificate of Incorporation (Registration Number: CG130341120) and Tax Identification Number (TIN: C0057243338117).
In Ghana, according to UNICEF, over 13 million children are orphans, and approximately 100 million others have lost one or both parents. These numbers reflect not just statistics but the faces of children who deserve the same opportunities and care as any other child. We firmly believe that by providing them with the love, support, and education they need, we can transform their lives and, in turn, contribute to a better Ghana.

At Smile Heart Ghana LBG, our unwavering dedication is guided by a set of clear objectives. Our mission is to find loving foster parents for orphaned and street children, ensuring they receive the same love, care, and opportunities as any other child. We are committed to reducing the number of orphaned and vulnerable children on the streets of Ghana’s cities, enrolling them in schools to provide them with quality education. Additionally, we actively support and strengthen other orphanages across the country. Through our charity work, we extend our helping hand to those in need throughout our society. These objectives drive us to make a meaningful impact on the lives of Ghana’s less fortunate children and contribute to building a brighter future for our nation.

Our Mission

Get foster parents for the orphans and street children in other for them to receive love and care as other children and doing charity works to support the needy.

Our Vision

Reduce the number of street children and orphans in cities of Ghana by 50% in the year 2030.

Our Achievements

Making a Positive Impact

Donations To Saviour Children Foundation At Kasoa, Adam Nana Central-region

We provided essential items Saviour children Foundation, supporting orphans in their daily lives and education. This included a computer, a standing fan, bags of rice, cooking oil, used clothes, shoes, bibles, and other food items.

Fed 1000 Street Children

On Christmas day in 2017, we distributed fried rice and chicken to approximately 1000 street children along the principal streets of Accra. This gesture aimed to bring joy to these children during the festive season.

Mwofo Book Project

We donated around 1000 exercise books, pens, pencils, rulers, erasers, and notebooks to Mwofo R/C in the Jirapa District of the Upper East Region of Ghana. This project was dedicated to promoting education within the community.

Donation to Ve Deme Orphanage

In Afadjato, in the Volta Region of Ghana, we donated items worth GHc 3000 to the Ve Deme Orphanage, also known as Volta Home. Our donation included essential supplies like rice, cooking oil, sugar, toiletries, and more, aiming to support and uplift the orphans living there.

Akraman Pads and Educational Material Donation

To support Akraman Catholic School in Anomabo at Cape Coast, we donated books, sanitary pads, pens, pencils, computers, and various IT items. This contribution aimed to address the school's need for essential educational resources.

Donation to Kobby, the Boy with Cerebral Palsy

In July 2020, we made a significant donation to Kobby, a six-year-old boy with Cerebral Palsy. This included a wheelchair to aid in his mobility, along with financial support for his ongoing care and medical expenses.

Donation for Daniel Takyi’s Surgery

We provided financial assistance of GHc 6000 for the surgery of Daniel Takyi, a young boy suffering from a recurring mandible condition. This support allowed him to receive the necessary medical treatment and lead a healthier life.

FAQs

Have some questions in mind?

Smile Heart Ghana is a nonprofit organization dedicated to providing care, education, and support to orphaned and vulnerable children in Ghana. Our mission is to create a safe and loving environment where children can grow, learn, and thrive, ensuring they have a bright future.

You can support us by donating funds, sponsoring a child, volunteering your time, or providing essential supplies such as food, clothing, and educational materials. We also welcome partnerships with individuals and organizations who share our vision of empowering children for a better future.

We prioritize the safety and well-being of our children by providing proper healthcare, education, emotional support, and mentorship. Our team of dedicated caregivers and social workers ensures that every child receives the necessary care, protection, and opportunities to succeed.

Yes, we welcome visitors and volunteers who wish to spend time with the children, assist with activities, or contribute to our programs. However, we have guidelines in place to ensure the safety and well-being of the children. Please contact us in advance to schedule a visit and learn more about our volunteer opportunities.

Every donation is used to directly support the children’s needs, including food, healthcare, education, and shelter. We also invest in skill development programs and extracurricular activities to empower the children for a self-sufficient future. Transparency is important to us, and we provide updates on how donations are utilized.
